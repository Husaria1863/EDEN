# EDEN Canon

This document defines what belongs in the official version of The EDEN Project.

## Core Concept

The EDEN Project is a collaborative early-world simulation built through community contributions.

The official project focuses on building a believable early Earth environment, early-human experience, and survival/simulation foundation.

## Current Setting

The project is currently focused on an early-human, pre-civilization world.

The exact historical/scientific scope may be refined over time, but the official project should avoid modern, fantasy, or unrelated content unless specifically approved.

## Allowed Content

Official contributions may include:

* Early humans
* Natural environments
* Terrain
* Weather
* Animals
* Plants
* Stone tools
* Fire
* Shelter
* Survival systems
* Environmental simulation
* Early-human interaction systems
* Research-backed worldbuilding

## Not Allowed in Official Canon

The official version should not include:

* Modern weapons (unless appropriate means to acquire these items are implemented)
* Modern buildings (unless appropriate means to acquire these items are implemented)
* Cars or vehicles (unless appropriate means to acquire these items are implemented)
* Sci-fi technology
* Magic systems
* Fantasy creatures
* Meme content
* Unrelated pop-culture references
* Features that break the early-world direction

## Forks

Forks may experiment freely, but not every forked idea belongs in the official version.

The official version is curated and must stay consistent with the project direction.

## Final Review

Maintainers decide whether a contribution fits official canon before it is merged.
